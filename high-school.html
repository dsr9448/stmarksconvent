High School

At St. Marks Convent, our high school program is designed to provide a well-rounded education that prepares students for academic excellence and future success. We foster an engaging and disciplined learning environment where students develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and leadership skills.

Optimized Class Strength

Focused teaching in a disciplined academic environment.

Well-Equipped Smart Classrooms

Technology-integrated lessons for better concept clarity.

Homework Support

Exam Preparation Support

Special attention to board exam readiness.

Skill Development Programs

Enhancing public speaking, leadership, and career guidance.

Practical Learning
