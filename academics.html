Curriculum Details

Academics

At St. Mark's Convent, we follow the State Board syllabus prescribed by the Karnataka Government, integrated with the National Education Policy (NEP) guidelines to provide a well-rounded and future-ready education. Our curriculum emphasises conceptual understanding, critical thinking, and practical application, ensuring that students develop both academic excellence and essential life skills.

Beyond academics, we offer a range of co-curricular and extracurricular activities to promote holistic development. Students have the opportunity to engage in Karate, Yoga, Dance, Shloka Recitation, and Abacus, vedic math, fostering discipline, creativity, and cognitive skills. Through this balanced approach, we strive to nurture confident, capable, and well-rounded individuals prepared for success in all aspects of life.

At St. Mark's Convent, we believe in:

  • Education Beyond Academics – Developing leadership and moral values.
  • Empowering Students – Building confidence and discipline.
  • A Nurturing Environment – Encouraging creativity and teamwork.

Our motto, "Knowledge is Power", reflects our unwavering commitment to shaping future leaders through quality education, ethics, and excellence.

Join us and experience the power of education!
