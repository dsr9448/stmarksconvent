Pre-School

At St. Marks Convent, we believe that early childhood is a crucial stage for learning and development. Our pre-school program provides a nurturing environment where children explore, discover, and grow through play-based and interactive learning.

Pre-School (Nursery, LKG, UKG)

Our Approach and Teaching Methodology

  • Play way method and experiential learning approach.
  • Focus on developing cognitive, motor, and social skills.
  • Safe, caring, and interactive environment designed to nurture curiosity.
  • Early introduction to foundational concepts in English, Mathematics, and Life Skills through stories, art, and play.
  • Trained and compassionate teachers specialising in early childhood education.
  • We use advanced audiovisual tools like projectors, digital presentations, and educational videos to enhance learning and retention.

Program Focus

  • Foundation for literacy and numeracy through playful methods.
  • Focus on building self-confidence, independence, and good habits.
  • Emotional and social development through group activities and storytelling.
  • Introduction to nature, music, and creative arts for all-around development.

Timings

  • Monday to Saturday: 9:00 AM – 12:30 PM
  • Flexible settling-in period for new toddlers.
  • Half-day option available for the initial weeks.

Uniform

  • Comfortable and colourful uniforms designed for free movement.
  • Shoes with Velcro for easy handling by little ones.
  • Extra set of clothes to be kept in the school for emergencies.

Note to Parents

We encourage parents to focus on their child’s emotional readiness rather than academic pressure.
Open communication with teachers is encouraged to understand your child’s progress.
Settling-in period is normal; every child adapts at their own pace.
