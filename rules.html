Rules

Zero Donation / Free Admission

RULES & GUIDELINES

Appearance & Dress Code Guidelines

  • Students must bring their School Diary and Identity Card daily for easy identification and record-keeping.
  • A well-groomed appearance is expected throughout school hours, reflecting discipline and respect.

Uniform Standards:

  • Attire should be tidy, wrinkle-free, and properly worn.
  • Shoes must be clean and polished, with appropriate socks.

Personal Cleanliness:

  • Students should maintain proper hygiene and present themselves neatly every day.

Dress Code for Girls:

  • Shirts should follow school specifications and extend adequately below the waistline.
  • Skirt length must be modest, reaching at least the knees.
  • Long hair should be tied back neatly; extravagant hair colors are not allowed.

Dress Code for Boys:

  • Shirts should be tucked in at all times for a sharp appearance.
  • Haircuts should be neat, short, and appropriate for a school environment.

Accessories Policy:

  • Students are advised not to wear valuable jewelry, fancy watches, or decorative wristbands to avoid loss or distraction.

SAFETY & PROHIBITED ITEMS

Items That Risk Safety:

  • Bringing sharp objects, weapons, fireworks, or any harmful tools to school is strictly prohibited.

Electronic Devices:

  • Personal gadgets like mobile phones, music players, or gaming devices are not allowed on the premises to ensure an undistracted learning environment.

Chewing Substances:

  • Consumption of chewing gum, pan, or similar products is strictly prohibited on school grounds.

CAMPUS BEHAVIOUR & CONDUCT

Respect for Property:

  • Students must handle school infrastructure, furniture, and books with care.
  • Any intentional damage will lead to disciplinary measures, and repair costs will be recovered from the student.

Responsible Behaviour:

  • Writing on walls, desks, or defacing school property is strictly prohibited.
  • Using inappropriate or offensive language is unacceptable and may result in suspension.

Classroom and Campus Etiquette:

  • Maintain silence during lessons and avoid crowding or loitering outside classrooms.
  • Running and shouting in corridors or common areas should be avoided.
  • While using staircases and hallways, students should keep to the left to ensure smooth movement.

Clean Environment:

  • Everyone is responsible for keeping the campus litter-free.
  • Waste should be disposed of in the bins provided.

Education is the most powerful tool for transformation. At St. Mark's Convent, we believe in nurturing young minds with knowledge, discipline.
